I was born in Central America, Granada, Nicaragua. I came to the United States when I was 12 years old. I remember that during that month of December, the weather was very cold. I lived in the Bronx for most of my life, but I also lived in other boroughs and other states.

I always had a passion for Illustration. During my teen years, I used illustration as a way to express my thoughts and ideas. I never had the opportunity of having someone to teach me new techniques as I tried to enhance my drawing skills. I looked at other peoples work like paintings, sketches, cartoons and comic book drawings to study their styles. Some of these great artists are Alex Ross, Frank Frazetta, Jim Lee and Frank Miller. At age of 33, I attended FIT University of New York City, I was able to study Graphic Design, Illustration and Photography.

At times, the language has been a barrier for me, this barrier hasn't stop me from learning. I was able to complete my seculiar education on time and learned new things. This has helped me to improve as a person and reach my goals. Web Developing is a new goal that I have and I want to excel in this new career. I want to use my creativity as I build attractive websites and make all of my clients very happy with the work I have done for them.
